## PRODUCTION BIBLE

### VISUAL STYLE
- **Color grade**: Crushed blacks, teal/orange highlights, cyberpunk green/red flashes
- **Text font**: Bold condensed (Bebas Neue or Impact), ALL CAPS, glitch/shake effect
- **Transitions**: Hard cuts, glitch wipes, zoom punches, RGB split, VHS distortion
- **Screen recordings**: Dark terminal, neon accent glow, zoomed into key data with cursor movement
- **Stock footage sources**: Pexels, Artgrid — military, explosions, oil rigs, trading floors, night cities, servers

### AUDIO STYLE
- Deep bass drops on reveals (808 boom)
- Phonk / dark trap beats (royalty-free)
- ElevenLabs AI voice — deep, cold, commanding
- Sound FX: notification pings, cash register, alarm sirens, typing, heartbeat
- Use trending audio when it fits — adapt concept to sound

### REEL STRUCTURE (every reel follows this)
```
0.0s — HOOK (text or voice, max 3 seconds)
0.5s — PATTERN INTERRUPT (visual shock, bass drop, zoom)
3.0s — PAYLOAD (the actual content, proof, demo)
8.0s — TWIST or ESCALATION
12.0s — CTA (text overlay, not spoken)
```
